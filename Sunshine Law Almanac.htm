  1. Local Journalist

    You start knowing 1 not-in-play character of each type.

    “Extra! Extra! Famous singer suddenly moves to London, no one knows why!”

    The Journalist learns a not-in-play character of each type.

    • During the first night, the Local Journalist is woken and shown the character tokens of one Townsfolk, one Outsider, one Minion, and one Demon, none of which are in play.
    • They learn this only once and then learn nothing more.

    Examples

    The Local Journalist learns the Hitman, VIP, Kingpin, and Illegal since none of them are in play.

    In a 12 player game, the Agitator and Propagandist are both in play, causing there to be four outsiders. The Local Journalist learns that the Socialite, Terrorist, and Spymaster are not in play.

    On the first night, the Kingpin poisons the Local Journalist, The Local Journalist learns the Police Officer, VIP, Kingpin, and Volkspolizei, even though the Police Officer and Kingpin are in play

    How to Run

    During the first night, wake the Local Journalist. Show the Local Journalist the character tokens of a not-in-play character of each type (Townsfolk, Outsider, Minion and Demon). Put the Local Journalist to sleep.

    Townsfolk

  2. Socialite

    You start knowing one of two players is a particular character. While that character is alive, you cannot die at night.

    “You don't scare me, do you know the connections I have? I am untouchable.”

    The Socialite learns that a specific character is in play, and is safe from the Demon while that character is alive.

    • During the first night, the Socialite is woken, shown two players, and learns the character of one of them.
    • They learn this only once and then learn nothing more.
    • If the Demon attacks the Socialite while the character they were shown is still alive then the Socialite does not die. The Demon does not get to attack another player—there is simply no death tonight.
    • The Socialite is not protected against the Demon nominating and executing someone.

    Examples

    Michelson is the FBI Analyst, and Daniel is the Mole. The Socialite learns that Michelson or Daniel is the FBI Analyst. Later, the Spymaster attacks the Socialite while Michelson is alive. No death occurs tonight.

    Jordan is the ITAR Enforcer, and Jerry is the Propagandist. The Socialite learns that Jordan or Jerry is the Propagandist. Later, the Illegal attacks the Socialite while Jerry is alive. No death occurs tonight.

    Gregory is the Illegal and receives the DEA Agent, and John is the Informant. The Socialite learns that Gregory or John is the DEA Agent. Later, Gregory attacks the Socialite, the Socialite might die, depending if Gregory registers as the DEA Agent.

    How to Run

    While preparing the first night, put the Socialite's CONNECTION and WRONG reminder tokens by any other character tokens.

    During the first night, wake the Socialite and point to the players marked CONNECTION and WRONG. Show the character token marked CONNECTION to the Socialite. Put the Socialite to sleep.

    During the night, if the player marked CONNECTION is alive and the Socialite would die, the Soldier remains alive.

    You should usually make the CONNECTION a good player, but you can occasionally make it a Minion for a more interesting dynamic. You should not make the CONNECTION a Demon without a very good reason

    Townsfolk

  3. Informant

    Each night, learn one good player. The demon knows you are in play and guesses each night. If the demon picks you, you are poisoned.

    “It is only a matter of time before they find me, I will pass on what I can before that.”

    The Informant learns good players in decreasing order of reliability.

    • Each night, the Informant is woken and learns a good player. It can be a player they already learned.
    • The shown player can be alive or dead.
    • Each night, the Demon wakes after the Informant and picks a player. If the Demon picks the Informant, the Informant is poisoned for the rest of the game. Neither the Demon nor the Informant learn this has happened.
    • If the Informant is drunk or poisoned, they may learn good or evil players.

    Examples

    Steph and Jerry are good; Leon and Jett are evil. On the first night, the Informant learns Jerry. On the second night, the Informant learns Steph but the Demon guesses the Informant. On the third night, the Informant learns Leon. On the fourth night, the Informant learns Jett. On the fifth night, the informant learns Steph again.

    How to Run

    Each night, wake the Informant. Point to a good player. Put the Informant to sleep.

    Each night, after you put the Informant to sleep, wake the Demon. The Demon points at any player. Put the demon to sleep. If the chosen player is the Informant, put the POISONED token by the Informant character token.

    If good is winning, and the Informant is not poisoned, you can help the evil team by giving the Informant a player they have already received.

    Townsfolk

  4. FBI Analyst

    Each night you learn how many players switched character or alignment since dawn.

    “For the last six months, he’s gone to Harvard and Berkeley. I’m betting he can get a passport.”

    The FBI Analyst knows how many players have changed or defected since dawn today.

    • When a player changes characters or changes alignment, the FBI Analyst will learn that it happened.
    • The FBI Analyst does not learn which players switch, only to how many players it happened.
    • If a player changes both character and alignment, they only count once for the FBI Analyst.

    Examples

    Michael is claiming to have been turned into a Useful Idiot by the Propagandist. Michelson is the FBI Analyst and receives a 0, so thinks Michael is lying.

    Kevin is turned into a Useful Idiot by the Propagandist, during the same night, Minh, the Local Journalist submits to the Volkspolizei's blackmail and becomes an evil Volkspolizei. The FBI Analyst learns a 2

    How to Run

    Each time a player changes character or alignment (or both), mark their character token with a SWITCHED reminder.

    Each night, wake the FBI Analyst Show fingers (0, 1, 2, etc.) equaling the number of characters with SWITCHED reminders. Put the FBI Analyst to sleep. Remove all SWITCHED reminders.

    Townsfolk

  5. DEA Agent

    Each night, choose a player: they are sober & healthy until dusk. You learn the character the Kingpin picked last. If the Kingpin picks you, they die.

    “It's Easy Money - 'Til We Catch You.”

    The DEA Agent protects players from drunkenness and poison. They learn who the Kingpin picked last night, and kill the Kingpin if they attempt to sell the DEA Agent drugs.

    • Each night, the DEA Agent may choose to protect any player from drunkenness and poison.
    • If a player protected by the DEA Agent is drunk or poisoned, their ability still works.
    • Each night, the DEA Agent learns which character the Kingpin picked the night before. They do not learn which player the Kingpin picked.
    • If the Kingpin chooses the DEA Agent, they die.

    Examples

    Vivek is the Kingpin, Leon is the Local Journalist, and Gregory is the DEA Agent. Vivek poisons Leon. The next night, the Gregory learns that the Local Journalist was poisoned, that same night, Vivek tries to poison Gregory, Vivek dies.

    The Celebrity and is killed by the Demon, making all Minions drunk. There is no Kingpin in play, so the DEA Agent does not learn anything. The DEA Agent chooses Daniel who is the Propagandist, Daniel's ability works because they are sober and healthy.

    How to Run

    Each night, wake the DEA Agent. The DEA Agent points at any player. Put the Monk to sleep. Put the DEA Agent's SOBER AND HEALTHY reminder token by the chosen player's character token.

    A player marked SOBER AND HEALTHY is sober and healthy (even if they’re also marked DRUNK or POISONED) and always gets true information (even if an ability would make them drunk or poisoned).

    If the Kingpin poisons a player, put the DEA Agent's KINGPIN PICK reminder token by the poisoned player's character token.

    Each night except the first, if any character token is marked KINGPIN PICK, show that character token to the DEA Agent. Put the DEA Agent to sleep.

  6. ITAR Enforcer

    Each night, choose a character different to last night: that character, is drunk until dawn. Chosen opposing players learn who you are.

    “If we cut off the supply to the Russians, their operations will grind to a halt.”

    The ITAR Enforcer blocks characters from using their abilities.

    • Each night, the ITAR Enforcer chooses a character. If they choose a character who is in play, that character is drunk until dawn.
    • If the ITAR Enforcer chooses an opposing character who is in play, that characters learns that the ITAR Enforcer chose them, and who the ITAR Enforcer is.
    • The ITAR Enforcer may not choose the same character two nights in a row.

    Examples

    The ITAR Enforcer targets the Celebrity, the Celebrity is drunk until dawn.

    The ITAR Enforcer targets the Propagandist, no Propagandist is in play, so nothing happens.

    Maxwell is the ITAR Enforcer and targets the Spymaster. The Storyteller informs the Spymaster that they may not open their eyes that night and the Spymaster does not kill tonight. At dawn, the Storyteller declares that nobody died that night.

    The ITAR Enforcer is evil and targets the Hitman. The Hitman learns that the ITAR Enforcer chose them, and is drunk until dawn.

    How to Run

    Each night, wake the ITAR Enforcer. They point at any character icon on their character sheet. If that character is in play, mark the chosen character token with the ITAR Enforcer's DRUNK reminder. Put the ITAR Enforcer to sleep.

    If the ITAR Enforcer chose the an in play opposing character, wake the character they chose. Show them the THIS CHARACTER SELECTED YOU info token and the ITAR Enforcer character token, then point at the ITAR Enforcer player. Put them to sleep.

  7. SIGINT Analyst

    Each night, choose an alive player: you learn the distance to the nearest evil player.

    “The signal is much stronger over here, there must be a transmission source nearby.”

    The SIGINT Analyst learns the distance from a living player to the nearest evil player.

    • Each night, the SIGINT Analyst chooses a player and learns the distance from them to the nearest evil player.
    • The SIGINT Analyst does not learn if the chosen player is evil. If the chosen player is evil, the SIGINT Analyst learns the distance from them to the nearest other evil player.
    • The SIGINT Analyst can choose any living player, including themself. If the SIGINT Analyst chooses a dead player accidentally, the Storyteller prompts them to choose again.

    Examples

    The SIGINT Analyst chooses Kerry, the Informant. Clockwise from Kerry is Bradley the DEA Agent then Nathan the Terrorist. Anticlockwise from Kerry is Miguel the Police Officer, Han the VIP, and Alex the Agitator. The SIGINT Analyst learns a 2.

    The SIGINT Analyst chooses Ethan, the Kingpin. Clockwise from Ethan is Douglas the Mole. Anticlockwise from Ethan is Oliver the Hitman and Saiyam the Volkspolizei. The SIGINT Analyst learns a 2, because the Mole registers as good.

    The SIGINT Analyst chooses Andrew, the Mercenary. Andrew is sitting next Gregory, who is the evil Double Agent, so the SIGINT Analyst learns a 1.

    How to Run

    Each night, wake the SIGINT Analyst. They point at any player. Show fingers (1, 2, etc.) equaling the distance in players from the chosen player to the nearest evil player, starting with the player neighboring the chosen player closer to that evil player. Put the SIGINT Analyst to sleep.

    Townsfolk

  8. Police Officer

    Each night choose a player: They are safe from evil players tonight. If you choose the Terrorist, kidnapped players are released.

    “Treating victims with compassion is just as important as catching the criminals. They need to feel heard.”

    The Police Officer protects players from the evil team.

    • Each night, the Police Officer may choose to protect any player.
    • If an evil player targets a player who has been protected by the Police Officer, then that ability does not work. The evil player does not get to target another player.
    • The Police Officer does not protect against an evil player nominating and executing someone.

    Examples

    The Police Officer protects Adison, the Socialite. later, the Propagandist chooses Adison. Adison does not become a Useful Idiot because he is protected by the Police Officer.

    The Police Officer protects Steph, the ICBM Officer. later, the Spymaster attacks Steph. No deaths occur tonight.

    The John is the Terrorist and kidnaps Safwan. The Police Officer protects John, Safwan is released.

    How to Run

    Each night except the first, wake the Police Officer. The Police Officer points at any player Put the Police Officer to sleep. Put the Police Officer's SAFE reminder token by the chosen player's character token.

    If the Police Officer points to the Terrorist, and a player's character token is marked KIDNAPPED, wake that player, show them the Kidnapper character token and shake your head no. Put that player to sleep and remove the KIDNAPPED reminder token.

    If any evil player targets the player marked SAFE, do not do anything.

    Townsfolk

  9. Double Agent

    Each night*, you may change alignment, if you do, a player might die. Demons think you are a Minion, Minions think you are a Demon.

    “I could assure you that I am not disloyal to the cause, but then I might be not be being totally honest.”

    The Double Agent switches teams at will.

    • On the first night, instead of learning which player is the Demon, the Minions are told that both players—the Demon and the Double Agent—are the Demon.
    • On the first night, the Demon learns that the Double Agent player is one of its Minions.
    • The Double Agent does not wake to learn anything.
    • The Storyteller can point to the Double Agent and the evil players in any order, so that the evil players won’t know which player is the Magician.
    • Each night except the first, the Double Agent may switch teams.
    • If the Double Agent switches teams, a player might die. That player could even be the Double Agent, either before or after they switch.

    Examples

    The Minions wake to learn that either the Illegal player or the Double Agent player is the Demon. The Illegal player learns that the Mole player, the Terrorist player, and the Double Agent player are the Minions.

    The Double Agent switches to the evil team, a the Kingpin dies.

    The Double Agent switches to the good team team, the Double Agent dies before they switch and remains evil.

    How to Run

    During the first night, wake the Minions. Show them the THIS IS THE DEMON info token, then point to the Demon and the Double Agent. Put the Minions to sleep.

    During the first night, wake the Demon. Show the THESE ARE YOUR MINIONS info token, then point to all Minions and the Double Agent. Show the THESE CHARACTERS ARE NOT IN PLAY info token and three character tokens. Put the Demon to sleep.

    Do this instead of the normal Minion Info and Demon Info steps.

    Townsfolk

  10. ICBM Officer

    Each night*, if the there is more than 1 extra evil player, choose 2 players: Chosen evil players die. You are sober & healthy.

    “We are go for launch. Awaiting final confirmation.”

    The ICBM Officer deters the evil team from expanding.

    • The ICBM Officer only acts if at least two more players than normal are evil.
    • The normal number of evil players is printed on the Traveller sheet and on the Setup sheet.
    • If the ICBM Officer wakes, they choose 2 players. Any evil players the ICBM Officer chooses dies.
    • If the ICBM Officer is immune to being drunk and poisoned.

    Examples

    The Propagandist makes an Outsider evil and the Double Agent switches teams. Tomorrow night, the ICBM Officer targets the Propagandist and the Double Agent, they both die.

    There are two extra evil players. The ICBM Officer chooses the Terrorist and the Celebrity. Only the Terrorist dies.

    There are three extra evil players. The ICBM Officer chooses the Spymaster and the Mole. The Mole registers as good, but the Spymaster dies and good wins.

    There are two extra evil players. The Kingpin has picked the ICBM Officer and is still alive. The ICBM Officer chooses the Hitman and the Kingpin, the Kingpin dies because the ICBM Officer cannot be made drunk or poisoned.

    How to Run

    Each night, if there is more than 1 extra evil player, wake the ICBM Officer. The ICBM Officer points at any two players. If either chosen player is evil, chosen evil players die.

    Townsfolk

  11. Hitman

    One per game at night, pick a player: They learn that you picked them. If you picked an opposing player last night, they die.

    “Target neutralized.”

    The Hitman spends a day tracking a target and assassinates them.

    • The Hitman chooses a player one night and, If that player is on the other team, kills them on the next night.
    • The alignment check happens on the second night, if the player is on the same team as the Hitman when they are chosen, but then switches teams before the Hitman acts on the second night, they die. If they were on the opposite team to the Hitman when chosen, but switches teams before the Hitman acts on the second night, they do not die.
    • If the Hitman is poisoned on either night when they act, the target does not die and the Hitman loses their ability.

    Examples

    Jerry is the Hitman and chooses Gerard, the Propagandist, the next night, Gerard dies.

    Nathan is the Hitman and chooses Michael, the Celebrity, nothing happens.

    Han is the Hitman and chooses Saiyam, the Kingpin. The next night, Saiyam poisons Han. Saiyam does not die.

    Eric is the Hitman and chooses Daniel, the good Double Agent. Later that night, Daniel changes to the evil team. The next night, Daniel dies.

    How to Run

    Each night, wake the Hitman. They either shake their head no or point at any player.

    If they shake their head no, nothing happens. Put the Hitman to sleep.

    If they point at a player, put the Hitman to sleep. Mark the chosen player with the MARKED reminder.

    If a player had the MARKED reminder at the start of the night, and are on the opposite team to the Hitman, they die. Remove the MARKED reminder and put the Hitman's NO ABILITY reminder token by the Hitman character token.

  12. Celebrity

    If you die at night, all minions are drunk for 2 nights & 2 days.

    “MIAMI, ARE YOU READY TO PARTY?!”

    The Celebrity, when they die, causes Minions to be drunk for a while.

    • If the Celebrity dies at night, all Minions are made drunk for two nights and two days, starting immediately.
    • Dead Minions are also affected by the Celebrity's ability.
    • If the Celebrity is poisoned when they die, Minions are not made drunk, even if the Celebrity later becomes sober and healthy.
    • If the Celebrity made Minions drunk, but the Celebrity becomes drunk or poisoned, the Minions become sober again. If the Celebrity becomes sober and healthy again before the two nights and two days have ended, Minions become drunk yet again.

    Examples

    During the night, the Celebrity dies. For the rest of this night and the next night, the Minions wake up but abilities do not work.

    The Celebrity dies at night while drunk. Nothing happens.

    How to Run

    If the Celebrity dies at night,all Minions become drunk—put the Celebrity’s MINIONS DRUNK 1 reminder in the center of the left side of the Grimoire. The next night, replace the MINIONS DRUNK 1 reminder with the MINIONS DRUNK 2 reminder. At dusk on the next night, remove the MINIONS DRUNK 2 reminder.

  13. CIA Officer

    The first time an opposing player targets you, that player is made drunk until dusk and you gain their ability until dusk.

    “Perhaps you can take this package elsewhere, and we can keep this incident between us.”

    The CIA Officer turns the first enemy that targets them into an asset.

    • The first time an opposing player targets the CIA Officer, they are made a CIA Asset before their ability activates until dusk. CIA Assets are always drunk.
    • The CIA Officer learns which character selected them and may use their ability tonight or tomorrow, whenever the CIA Asset player would normally use it.
    • If the CIA Asset has a passive effect, the CIA Officer gains this effect until dusk.
    • The CIA Officer does not learn which player selected them.
    • If the CIA Officer is drunk or poisoned the first time an opposing player targets them, the opposing player's ability works normally and the CIA Officer loses their ability.

    Examples

    Jericho is the Propagandist and John is the CIA Officer. Jericho chooses John and is made Drunk. John learns that he has the Propagandist ability and picks Gregory, the Spymaster. Gregory turns into a Useful Idiot and, as there is no living Demon in the game, good wins.

    Vince is the Terrorist and Safwan is the CIA Officer. Vince chooses Safwan and is made Drunk. Safwan learns that he has the Terrorist ability and picks Alex, the Mercenary. Alex is kidnapped and learns this. At dusk, Safwan loses the Terrorist ability and Alex is released, Alex learns that he has been released.

    How to Run

    The first time an opposing player targets the CIA asset with their ability, put the CIA Officer's CIA ASSET and DRUNK reminder token by that player's character token before their ability activates.

    Show the CIA Officer the THIS CHARACTER HAS SELECTED YOU and the character token marked with the CIA ASSET reminder token. The CIA Officer may act as if they have the ability of the character marked with the CIA ASSET reminder token.

    Remove the CIA Officer's CIA ASSET and DRUNK reminder tokens at dusk

    Townsfolk

  14. Mercenary

    If you vote for & execute your target, you switch to the winning team at the end of the game.

    “If violence wasn't your last resort, you failed to resort to enough of it.”

    The Mercenary hunts their target, even at the expense of their team.

    • During the first night, the Mercenary is woken and shown a player. The show player is the Mercenary's target.
    • If the Mercenary's target is executed, and the Mercenary voted for their target, the Mercenary switches to the winning team at the end of the game.
    • The Mercenary only switches to the winning team if they are alive when the game ends.

    Examples

    The Mercenary nominates and votes for their target. This nomination is successful and the target is executed. Later in the game, the VIP dies and guesses both of their neighbours incorrectly. Evil wins, the Mercenary switches to the evil team and wins too.

    The Mercenary's target is killed at night. Later in the game, the Demon is executed. Good, the Mercenary wins too.

    The Mercenary nominates and votes for their target. Later, the Mercenary dies. Evil wins and the Mercenary loses, because their ability cannot activate to switch their team if they are dead.

    How to Run

    During setup, Mark one player with the TARGET reminder.

    During the first night, wake the Mercenary. Point to the player marked TARGET. Put the Mercenary to sleep.

    If the Mercenary votes for the nomination where player marked TARGET is executed, mark the Mercenary with the MISSION SUCCESS reminder.

    If, when the game ends, the Mercenary is marked with the MISSION SUCCESS reminder and is on the losing team, declare that the Mercenary changes teams and wins too.

    You should usually make the target a Townsfolk, presenting the Mercenary with a choice between trying to win by helping the good team, or trying to win by killing their target.

  15. VIP

    When you die, publically guess your 2 neighbour's characters, if both are incorrect, your team looses.

    “At all times I am protected by my bodyguards; Ben Hawkins and...what was your name again?.”

    The VIP must be flanked by at least one trustworthy bodyguard.

    • When the VIP dies, they guess their neighbors characters. They may take a few minutes to do so—after all, it’s a big decision, and other players may give advice on what to guess, but it is always the VIP’s decision. If they are wrong on both counts, the game ends immediately and the VIP's team loses. If they guess at least one right, nothing happens and the game continues.
    • It is not the Storyteller’s responsibility to prompt the VIP to declare they are the VIP and choose a player. The VIP must do this shortly after they learn that they are dead. Deliberately not doing so is considered cheating.

    Examples

    The VIP dies by execution. The VIP's clockwise neighbour is the Propagandist, and their anticlockwise neighbour is the Socialite. After much yelling and confusion, the VIP guesses their clockwise neighbour is the ITAR Enforcer and that their anticlockwise neighbour is the Socialite. Night falls, and the game continues.

    The Demon kills Saiyam, the VIP. Saiyam's clockwise neighbour, Vivek, is the Terrorist, and has convinced Saiyam that he is the Celebrity. Saiyam's anticlockwise neighbour is Han, the Informant. In the morning, when the Storyteller informs the group that Saiyam is dead, Saiyam says "Ok everybody, I was the VIP" the town discusses for a few minutes, Han does not want to reveal that he is the Informant. Saiyam publicly guesses that Vivek is the Celebrity and Han is the Propagandist. The evil team wins.

    How to Run

    When the VIP player is declared dead, they must declare that they are the VIP and then publicly guess their two neighbour's characters. (Give them time.) If guess both wrong, the game ends and the VIP's team loses. Otherwise, the game continues.

    If the VIP player doesn’t realize that they must choose a player when they die, privately remind them. New players may not understand how their character works.

    Outsider

  16. Chinese Consul

    If you are nominated today, tonight you become the opposite alignment of the nominator, you do not learn your alignment.

    “Keep a cool head and maintain a low profile. Never take the lead - but aim to do something big.”

    The Chinese Consul watches events from afar, not fully understanding what is happening, but knowing who their enemies are..

    • If a player nominates the Chinese Consul, tonight, if alive, the Chinese Consul will change alignment to oppose the player that nominated them.
    • The Chinese Consul may nominate themself, if they do, they will switch teams that night.

    Examples

    The Chinese Consul refuses to reveal who they are. The SIGINT Analyst nominates the Chinese Consul but the vote fails. That night, the Chinese Consul turns evil, but does not learn this.

    The Chinese Consul has not been nominated but believe evil is winning. The Chinese Consul nominates themself, but the vote fails. That night, the Chinese Consul turns evil.

    The Chinese Consul has turned themself evil, but now thinks the good team is winning so nominates themself to turn back to good. Good catches on and votes to execute the Chinese Consul. The Chinese Consul is not alive tonight, so does not switch alignment.

    How to Run

    If the Chinese Consul is nominated by a player on their team, mark them with the CHANGE ALIGNMENT reminder.

    At night, if the Chinese Consul is marked with the CHANGE ALIGNMENT reminder, flip the Chinese Consul character token to represent that they have changed alignment. Do not tell the Chinese Consul this has happened.

    Outsider

  17. Useful Idiot

    You are "mad" that one of your neighbours is evil, otherwise you or either of your neighbours might die.

    “I have followed exactly the same line the whole of my adult life. The fight against Fascism and the fight against imperialism were fundamentally the same fight.”

    The Useful Idiot accuses their neighbours.

    • “Madness” is a term that means “you are trying to convince the group of something.” So, if the Useful Idiot player is mad about one of their neighbours being evil, this means they are trying to convince people that one of their neighbours are evil.
    • This can be by verbally hinting they are evil, or by nominating a neighbour and giving a convincing reason. It is always up to the Storyteller to decide what the Useful Idiot is doing. If you think they are not trying hard enough to convince the group that that one of their neighbours is evil, you can execute the Useful Idiot or either of their neighbours, even outside the nomination phase, or at night. If you do, no other executions may happen today by normal means, since there is only one execution per day.
    • You may execute the Useful Idiot or one of their neighbours at night, even if an execution happened today. Declare they have died, and continue the night phase as normal. An execution may still happen the next day.
    • The Useful Idiot may switch which neighbour they think is evil. As long as they are making a convincing case that one of them is evil.

    Examples

    The Useful Idiot claims to be the SIGINT Analyst who picked themself and saw a 1. When challenged, they are emphatic in their claims that one of their neighbours is most likely evil due to their information, and so avoid death.

    The Useful Idiot neighbours the Informant and the SIGINT Analyst. The Useful Idiot claims that they trust both their neighbours, because they are powerful characters and the Useful Idiot wants to keep them alive. The Storyteller declares that the Informant dies.

    The Useful Idiot says their neighbour is evil, then says "By the way, I am definitely not the Useful Idiot." The Storyteller chooses to kill the Useful Idiot immediately.

    How to Run

    If the Useful Idiot is not mad that one of their neighbours is evil, you may kill the Useful Idiot or one of their neighbours.

    Outsider

  18. Mole

    You recieve three bluffs. Once per game, choose a good character: gain their ability if they are not in play. You might register as them.

    “The betrayal of trust carries a heavy taboo.”

    The Mole is very difficult for the good team to detect.

    • The Mole receives three not-in-play good character. These can be any three not-in-play good characters, including the Demon bluffs.
    • Once per game, the Mole can choose a good character. If that character is not in play, the Mole gains their ability.
    • If any character has an ability that would detect or affect a good player, then the Mole might register as good to that character. If any character has an ability that detects specific good characters, then the Mole might register as the character they chose. It is the Storyteller's choice as to what the Mole registers as, even a both alignments during the same night.
    • The Mole can only register as a good character once they have chosen one.

    Examples

    The Mole learns the VIP, the FBI Analyst, and the Hitman are not in play. The Mole chooses the Hitman ability and marks the Informant. The next night, the Informant dies.

    The Mole chooses the Informant, each night, they wake and learn a good player. The SIGINT Analyst chooses the Mole's neighbour, but gets a 3, because the Mole registers as good

    How to Run

    On the first night, show the Mole three good characters that are not in play.

    Each night, wake the Mole, they might choose a good character. If that character is not in play, swap the Mole token with the chosen character token, and mark them with the Is Mole reminder token. Put the Mole to sleep.

    The Mole might privately choose a good character during the day, If that character is not in play, swap the Mole token with the chosen character token, and mark them with the Is Mole reminder token.

    The Mole may choose their good character publicly if they wish.

    Once the Mole has picked a good character, stop waking them tonight as the Mole, instead, wake them at night when the character they chose would wake.

    If the Mole has picked a good character, each time the Mole is targeted by an ability that detects or affects good characters, choose what character and alignment the Mole registers as. (Do whatever is appropriate, such as showing a good character token, nodding, giving finger signals, or allowing an ability to work that would normally only affect good, Townsfolk, or Outsider players.)

    Avoid giving the Mole an especially important good character for the balance of the selected players. For example, if there are multiple characters that can expand the evil team, avoid giving the Mole the ICBM Officer.

  19. Propagandist

    Each night choose a player: they become a Useful Idiot until you pick next. The first Outsider you pick becomes evil. [+1 Outsider]

    “It would not be impossible to prove with sufficient repetition and a psychological understanding of the people concerned that a square is in fact a circle. They are mere words, and words can be molded until they clothe ideas and disguise.”

    The Propagandist brainwashes players, temporarily turning them into Useful Idiots.

    • Each time the Propagandist chooses a player they become a Useful Idiot until dusk.
    • At dusk, if the Propagandist does not choose them again, a chosen player reverts back to their previous character, even if the Propagandist is drunk, poisoned, or dead.
    • The first time the Propagandist chooses an Outsider, that Outsider changes to the Propagandist's alignment. The chosen outsider turns into a Useful Idiot until dusk, but remains evil after they revert to their previous character.
    • The newly evil player does not learn which players are Minions or the Demon.
    • There is an extra Outsider in play.

    Examples

    Daniel is the Propagandist and chooses Sachit, the Socialite. Sachit becomes the Useful Idiot and learns this. During the next night, Daniel chooses Sachit again, Sachit is not woken. During the third night, Daniel chooses Dylan, the VIP. Sachit is woken and learns that he is the Socialite. Dylan is woken and learns that he is the Evil Useful Idiot. During the third day, Daniel is executed. Dylan is woken and learns that he is now the VIP. He remains evil.

    Rithesh is the Propagandist and, on the first night chooses Henry, the Chinese Consul. Henry turn into the evil Useful Idiot. Henry learns that he has turned into the Useful Idiot, but does not learn they turned evil. On the second night, Rithesh chooses Henry again, Henry learns nothing. On the third night, Rithesh chooses Miguel, the Mercenary. Henry turns into an evil Chinese Consul. Henry learns that he turns into a Chinese consul, but not that he is evil. Miguel turns into the Useful Idiot but remains good, Miguel learns this. Later on the third night, the Demon kills the Celebrity, making Rithesh drunk. Rithesh chooses Miguel again. Miguel turns back into the Mercenary and learns this.

    How to Run

    While setting up the game, remove any one Townsfolk character token and add any one Outsider character token. This Outsider token goes into the bag instead of the Townsfolk token.

    Each night, wake the Propagandist. They point at any player. Put the Propagandist to sleep.

    If the Propagandist chose the same character as last time, put the Propagandist back to sleep.

    If the Propagandist chooses a different player to last time, wake the player marked with the IS USEFUL IDIOT reminder token. Show them the YOU ARE info token and their original character token, even if the Propagandist is drunk, poisoned, or dead.

    If the chosen player is the first Outsider the Propagandist chose, they become evil. Wake them and show them the YOU ARE info token and Useful Idiot character token. If they are not the Chinese Consul, give them a thumbs down, then put them to sleep. Flip their token upside down to represent that they are evil.

    If the chosen player is not an Outsider, or the Propagandist has already chosen an outsider, wake the chosen player. Show them the YOU ARE info token and the Useful Idiot character token, then put them to sleep. Put the IS USEFUL IDIOT reminder token next to their character token.

  20. Kingpin

    Each night, you may choose a player: they are poisoned. If you pick a demon, you die. You and the evil team do not learn eachother.

    “I'm a decent man who exports flowers.”

    The Kingpin sells drugs to players, poisoning them as long as the Kingpin keeps selling.

    • The Kingpin does not learn who the Demon and other Minions are. The Demon and other Minions do not see the Kingpin as on their team.
    • Each night, the Kingpin chooses a player, they are poisoned until the Kingpin dies.
    • If the Kingpin chooses the Demon, the Kingpin dies.
    • If the Kingpin is poisoned when the choose a player, that player is not poisoned until the Kingpin chooses them again, even if the Kingpin later becomes sober and healthy.
    • If the Kingpin becomes drunk or poisoned, all players poisoned become healthy. If the Kingpin becomes sober and healthy again, all players poisoned by the Kingpin become poisoned again.

    Examples

    There is a Mole and an Illegal in play. The Illegal only learns the Mole, and Mole only learns the Illegal.

    On the first night, the Kingpin chooses the SIGINT Analyst, who is poisoned. On the second night, the Kingpin chooses the Socialite, who is poisoned. On the third night, the Kingpin chooses the Agitator, the Kingpin dies. The SIGINT Analyst and the Socialite are no longer poisoned.

    How to Run

    During the first night, when you wake the Minions to show them the Demon, do not wake the Kingpin. When you make the Demon to show them the Minions, do not show them the Kingpin.

    Each night, wake the Kingpin. They point at any player. If the chosen player is the Demon, the Kingpin dies. Otherwise mark the chosen player with the Kingpin's POISONED reminder.

    Players the Kingpin poisons remain poisoned until the Kingpin dies. When the Kingpin dies, remove all of the Kingpin's POISONED reminders.

    If the Kingpin is made drunk or poisoned, all players poisoned due to the Kingpin become healthy. When the Kingpin becomes healthy again, all players they poisoned become poisoned again. Players the Kingpin attempted to poison while drunk or poisoned do not become poisoned until the Kingpin chooses them while sober and healthy.

  21. Terrorist

    Each night*, You may kill, release, or hold a kidnapped player. If no player is kidnapped, choose a player: unless the target guesses you, they will be kidnapped.

    “After 43 days we have ended his pitiful and corrupt existence. His death is meaningless to our pain and our rage, the struggle has only begun. Freedom through armed, anti-imperialist struggle.”

    The Terrorist kidnaps, and sometimes kills other players.

    • Each night, if the Terrorist does not have a kidnapped player at the start of the night, the Terrorist may attempt to kidnap a player.
    • The player the Terrorist attempts to kidnap learns this, and guesses who the Terrorist is. If they guess incorrectly, they are kidnapped, otherwise, nothing happens.
    • If the Terrorist has a kidnapped player at the start of the night, they have three options. If the Terrorist kills the player, the player dies, and is then released. If the Terrorist releases the player, the player is no longer kidnapped. If the Terrorist holds the player, the player remains kidnapped.
    • Players learn when they are kidnapped or released. Kidnapped players cannot use their ability, and do not wake to use their ability if they normally would.
    • If the Terrorist is drunk or poisoned, they hold any kidnapped players. If a drunk Terrorist attempts to kidnap a player, that player does not wake to guess the Terrorist and does not become kidnapped.

    Examples

    On the first night, the Terrorist chooses the Police Officer. The Police Officer wakes and chooses the VIP. The Police Officer is kidnapped and learns this.

    On the third night, the Terrorist chooses the Local Journalist. The Local Journalist wakes and chooses the Terrorist. Nothing happens.

    On the second night, the Terrorist has kidnapped the FBI Agent and chooses to kill. The FBI Agent is killed, then released. The FBI Agent learns that they were released.

    On the fourth night, the Terrorist has kidnapped the ITAR Enforcer and chooses to release. The ITAR Enforcer is released and learns this.

    On the second night, the Terrorist has kidnapped the Hitman and chooses to hold. Nothing happens.

    How to Run

    Each night except the first, wake the Terrorist. If there is a player currently marked KIDNAPPED, the Terrorist chooses to kill, release or hold. Put the Terrorist to sleep.

    If the Terrorist chooses to kill, wake the KIDNAPPED player, show the Kidnapper character token and shake your head no. put the KIDNAPPED player to sleep. Kill the KIDNAPPED player and remove the KIDNAPPED reminder.

    If the Terrorist chooses to release, wake the KIDNAPPED player, show the Kidnapper character token and shake your head no. put the KIDNAPPED player to sleep. Remove the KIDNAPPED token.

    If the Terrorist chooses to hold, do nothing

    If there is no player currently marked KIDNAPPED, the Terrorist points at a player. Put the Terrorist to sleep. Wake the chosen player, show them the THIS CHARACTER HAS SELECTED YOU info token and the Terrorist Character token. The chosen player points at a player, if they point at the Terrorist, nothing happens, otherwise, show them the Kidnapper token and nod your head yes, put the chosen player to sleep. Mark the chosen player with the KIDNAPPED token.

  22. Spymaster

    Each night*, choose a player: they die. You may choose to open your eyes at night.

    “Доброе утро Mr Johnson, could you explain to me why a simple journalist would require all that specialised radio equipment?”

    The Spymaster is aware of everything that happens at night.

    • The Spymaster may open their eyes at any point during the night, for as long or as short a time as they wish, they may open their eyes fully, or just peek.
    • When multiple players have their eyes open at once, they may communicate if they wish.
    • If a good player catches the Spymaster with their eyes open, there is no mechanical effect.
    • A dead Spymaster may not open their eyes at night. A drunk or poisoned Spymaster is told by the Storyteller that they may not open their eyes that night.

    Examples

    During the first night the Spymaster peeks at other players, and notices which good players wake and which do not. During the second night, the Spymaster opens their eyes more fully and notices that Jerry was woken and pointed to a player tonight and learned a number. During the 3rd night, Spymaster wakes and kills Jerry, whom the Spymaster believes is the SIGINT Analyst.

    How to Run

    Each night except the first, wake the Spymaster. They point at any player. That player dies.

    If the Spymaster loses their ability while alive, privately tell the Spymaster that they may not choose to open their eyes tonight.

  23. Volkspolizei

    Each night*, choose a player: they die. Once per game, the Volkspolizei may choose a second player. The second player can kill you and become an evil Volkspolizei, or they might be executed tomorrow.

    “Ve vould hate for zeze tapes to be made publik, perhaps ein Harrangement kould be made, ja?”

    The Volkspolizei blackmails other players.

    • Once per game, after the Volkspolizei chooses who to kill, they may choose a second player to blackmail.
    • The player the Volkspolizei blackmails can choose to submit or refuse. If they submit, the Volkspolizei dies and the blackmail subject becomes an evil Volkspolizei. If they refuse, they might be executed tomorrow.
    • Only the first Volkspolizei can blackmail another player, and they can only do it once per game.
    • If the Volkspolizei is drunk or poisoned when they attempt to blackmail, the target is not woken and the Volkspolizei cannot blackmail again.

    Examples

    On the third night, the Volkspolizei chooses the VIP and the Police Officer. The VIP dies. The Police Officer is woken and accepts. The Volkspolizei dies and the Police Officer becomes the new evil Volkspolizei.

    On the second night, the Volkspolizei chooses the Hitman and the Mercenary. The Hitman is protected by the Police Officer and does not die. The Mercenary is woken and refuses. The next day, the Mercenary is executed.

    On the fifth night, the Volkspolizei chooses the SIGINT Analyst and the Celebrity. The Volkspolizei is drunk, so the SIGINT Analyst survives and the Celebrity is not woken.

    On the first night, the Volkspolizei chooses the Local Journalist and the DEA Agent. The Local Journalist died. The DEA Agent is protected by the Police Officer and is not woken.

    How to Run

    Each night except the first, wake the Volkspolizei. They point at any player. That player dies.

    If the Volkspolizei's ONCE reminder is not in the centre of the Grimoire, after choosing who to kill, the Volkspolizei may point at another player. Put the Volkspolizei to sleep. Put the Volkspolizei's ONCE reminder in the center of the Grimoire. Wake the second player the Volkspolizei and show them the THIS CHARACTER SELECTED YOU info token and the Volkspolizei character token. If the second player nods yes, the Volkpolizei dies and the chosen player becomes an evil Volkspolizei. Otherwise, mark the second player with the REFUSED reminder. (Unlike “Once per game” abilities, this ONCE reminder stays there for the rest of the game. Don’t remove it, even if the Volkspolizei dies or changes character.)

    Players marked with the REFUSED reminder might be executed. At dusk, remove all REFUSED reminders.

    If the Volkpolizei uses their second choice when there are 3 players left alive and the target refuses, only execute the target if everyone knows who the Volkspolizei is. It is more fun for everyone to have a tense final day rather than end the game by Storyteller choice.

  24. Agitator

    Each night*, choose 2 players: the first dies, the second becomes evil until dusk. The first time an Agitator dies, if there is an evil outsider, an evil outsider becomes an evil Agitator.[-1 Minion, +1 Outsider, all Useful Idiots are evil]

    “El pueblo cubano vencerá a los imperialistas. ¡Viva la revolución!”

    The Agitator inspires other players to join their cause.

    • Each night, in addition to choosing a player to die, the Agitator also chooses a player to make evil until dusk.
    • At dusk, a the converted player reverts back to their original alignment, even if the Agitator is drunk, poisoned, or dead.
    • The first time the Agitator died, if there is an evil Outsider in play, an evil Outsider becomes an evil Agitator and does not revert to good even if they were made evil by an Agitator.
    • If the Agitator is drunk or poisoned when they die, an evil Outsider does not become an evil Agitator, even if one is in play.
    • During setup, replace one Minion token with an Outsider token.

    Examples

    The Agitator chooses the Hitman and the VIP. The Hitman dies and the VIP becomes evil. The next day, the Agitator is executed. The evil VIP becomes a new evil Agitator.

    John is the Agitator, Vivek is the Propagandist, and Leon is the Celebrity. Vivek chooses the Leon, who becomes an evil Useful Idiot. The next day, the John is executed. Leon becomes and remains the Agitator, even if Vivek chooses someone else tonight.

    How to Run

    While setting up the game, remove any one Minion character token and add any one Outsider character token. This Outsider token goes into the bag instead of the Minion token.

    All Useful Idiots in play at any point are made evil. If a Useful Idiot that is only evil due to the Agitator ceases to be a Useful Idiot, they are made good.

    At the start of the game, wake all Useful Idiots, show them the YOU ARE info token and give them a thumbs down. Flip their character token upside down to represent that they are evil.

    If a player is made a Useful Idiot due to the Propagandist, show them the YOU ARE info token, the Useful Idiot character token and give them a thumbs down. Flip their character token upside down to represent that they are evil and mark them with the IS USEFUL IDIOT reminder. If the IS USEFUL IDIOT reminder is removed, show them the YOU ARE info token, their original character token and give them a thumbs up. Flip their character token right side up to represent that they are good. Do this even if the Agitator is drunk, poisoned, or dead.

    Each night except the first, wake the Agitator. They point at any 2 players. Put the Agitator to sleep. The first player dies.

    If the Agitator's second choice is not marked with the Agitator's EVIL reminder token, wake the player marked with the Agitator's EVIL reminder token. Show them the YOU ARE info token and give them a thumbs up, Flip their character token right side up to represent that they are good. Do this even if the Agitator is drunk, poisoned, or dead.

    If the second player is good, wake them and show them the YOU ARE info token and give them a thumbs down. Flip their character token upside down to represent that they are evil and mark them with the Agitator's EVIL reminder token.

    If an Agitator dies, the Agitator's ONCE reminder is not in the center of the Grimoire, and there is at least one living evil Outsider, Put the Agitator's ONCE reminder in the center of the Grimoire. Wake a living evil Outsider. Show them the YOU ARE info token and the Agitator token. Change their character to an Agitator. Put the new Agitator to sleep. (Unlike “Once per game” abilities, this ONCE reminder stays there for the rest of the game. Don’t remove it, even if the Agitator dies or changes character.)

  25. Illegal

    Each night*, choose a player: they die. You get only 1 bluff. You gain your bluff's ability and might register as them.

    “I had a near death experience once; On a family trip to California in 1964. I was swimming at a beach and I nearly drowned.”

    The Illegal blends into the good team.

    • The Illegal only receives one not-in-play good character. The Illegal gains the ability of this good character, and may use it as if they were that character when that character would normally act.
    • If any character has an ability that would detect or affect a good player, then the Illegal might register as good to that character. If any character has an ability that detects specific good characters, then the Illegal might register as the character they received. It is the Storyteller's choice as to what the Illegal registers as, even a both alignments during the same night.

    Examples

    During Demon info, the Illegal learns the Hitman is not in play. During the second night, the Illegal chooses the ICBM Officer, they die. Later, the Illegal wakes again and chooses the Local Journalist. During the third night, the Local Journalist dies due to the Hitman ability.

    The SIGINT Analyst chooses the Illegal's neighbour but learns a 3. This is because the Illegal registered as their townsfolk.

    How to Run

    During Demon info, only show the Illegal one not-in-play good character. Replace the Illegal token with the not-in-play good character and mark them with the IS ILLEGAL reminder token. Don't forget to Flip their character token upside down to represent that they are evil. Put night tokens next to both the Illegal and the not-in-play good character.

    Each night, wake the Illegal when their not-in-play good character would wake. Show them their not-in-play good character's token and act as you would if they were that character.

    Each night except the first, wake the Illegal. They point at any player. That player dies.

    Each time the Illegal is targeted by an ability that detects or affects good characters, choose what character and alignment the Illegal registers as. (Do whatever is appropriate, such as showing a good character token, nodding, giving finger signals, or allowing an ability to work that would normally only affect good, Townsfolk, or Outsider players.)

    You should usually give the Illegal a character that the town would not want to execute. Otherwise, if the Illegal claims their character, they might be executed early, ending the game quickly.

  26. Kidnapper

    Player learn when they are kidnapped or freed. Kidnapped players are unable to use their ability.

    “You're going to be here for a while, so get comfortable. You'll have food, water, and a place to sleep. But don't think about trying to escape. It's not possible.”

    Use the Kidnapper in scripts that have a character that uses kidnapping.

    • Players learn when they are kidnapped or released.
    • Kidnapped players are unable to use their abilities. Players with abilities that would normally cause them to wake at night do not wake. Players with passive abilities lose their ability.

    Examples

    A player is kidnapped, they learn this.

    A player is released, they learn this.

    How to Run

    Whenever a player is kidnapped, mark them with the kidnapping character's KIDNAPPED token. Wake the kidnapped player, show them the Kidnapper character token and nod your head yes.

    Kidnapped players do not wake to use their ability. Passive abilities do not work. Players are unable to attempt to use "once per game" abilities when kidnapped.

    If a kidnapped player is released, wake that player, show them the Kidnapper character token and shake your head no. Remove the KIDNAPPED token. Do this even if the player being released is dead.
